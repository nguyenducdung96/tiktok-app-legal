Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-01

This Privacy Policy describes how "My TikTok Automation App" ("the App") handles information.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize the App via TikTok Login, we receive an OAuth access token and your TikTok open_id. We do NOT collect or store your TikTok password.

2. How We Use Information

We use the access token only to publish content on your behalf to your TikTok account, as authorized by you.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely in our automation system (n8n) and are used solely for the authorized purpose. We do not share data with third parties.

4. Data Retention

You can revoke access at any time via TikTok account settings. Tokens are deleted from our system upon revocation.

5. Third-Party Services

The App interacts only with the official TikTok API.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. Updates will be posted on this page.
